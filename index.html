Enter each party’s national vote share (numbers do not have to sum to 100). These values are normalized to yield adjusted national percentages that serve as the base vote for both parliamentary seat allocation and for distributing party support to presidential candidates.

Candidate Party Support

For each candidate, enter the percentage of each party’s vote they will receive (e.g. enter 100 for 100%—values larger than 100 are capped). In the final row (labeled Sandığa Gitmeyen) enter, for each party, the share of its vote that does not go to any candidate.
